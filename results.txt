SMEM vs DCache Thread Scaling Benchmark
=======================================

Configuration
-------------
- Threads swept from 1 to 32
- Synthetic miss latency used in this benchmark: 0 cycles
- Balanced runs spread requests across banks as evenly as possible
- Conflict runs force requests onto one bank
- DCache has 32 banks; SMEM has 32 banks

Mixed Hit/Miss
--------------
Each thread mixes hot read/write hits with two cold fetches and later reuses so every scenario includes both hits and misses.
